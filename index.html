ABOUT US

IBEXFLINT LTD (IBLTD) is based in London, United Kingdom, established in November 2020 and aims to provide convenience to shoppers by setting up a range of online shops. Our goal is to provide the average shopper with the best quality of products, the most affordable prices and the convenience of online shopping.

IBLTD aims to provide world-class service by placing its customers at the heart of its operations and making online shopping simpler and cheaper by providing state of the art online visuals, swift delivery at no cost to the customer.

Mission

We want to create value sustainably for our customers in providing a convenient and comfortable shopping environment with good service and quality products at reasonable prices.

Vision

Becoming the premier online retail shop in the Europe with our customer being at the heart of our operations.
